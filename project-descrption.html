IPASS-

  • I have Worked on this Project recently with my Client Company named Stellantis.
  • In this Project I have worked as a Scrum Master.
  • This Application is basically used to estimate cost for the shipping of parts from one place to another.
  • IPASS Provides the ability to investigate total Logistics/Variable Cost by direct sourcing of parts from supplier to the plants as requested from Purchasing.
  • Entire Scope of transportation cost is considered including Material handling, Logistics and International Sourcing.
